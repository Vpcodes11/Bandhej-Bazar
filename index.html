The Art of Bandhani

A Heritage of Excellence

Bandhani, also known as Bandhej, is an ancient tie-dye textile art form that has been practiced in Gujarat and Rajasthan for over 5,000 years. Each saree is a labor of love, with thousands of tiny knots tied by skilled artisans before dyeing.

The intricate patterns and vibrant colors represent centuries of tradition, passed down through generations. Every piece tells a story of cultural heritage and artistic mastery.

At Bandhej Bazar, we work directly with master craftsmen to bring you authentic, premium quality Bandhani sarees that celebrate this timeless art form.
